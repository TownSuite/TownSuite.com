TownSuite Land management

Land management is the easy to use yet powerful and comprehensive land management software.

Intuitive and robust planning featureset with connected GIS.

Streamline municipal land management. TownSuite Land management has several features to better manage your lifecycle.

Land management. Simplified.

Complete

One of the few applications built from the ground up for municipalities with hundreds of features added annually.

Connected

Everything linked in one source. Processes to ensure your data is correct and remains that way. External information included.

Control who does what

Connect to Asset Management, Finance, GIS, HRIS, Event & Facilities Management, and eBilling and payments - all from one source.

Parcel centric

Everything works in real time, and everything works the way you want it to since it is configurable to how you do things.

TownSuite Land management features include:

Building permits

From Initial Application - Including Pre-Application Plan Reviews through to To Final Inspection and/or Certificate of Occupancy. You define your process.

Development permits

Demolition permits

Occupancy permits

Sign permits

Building permits inspections

Print Daily Inspection reports by Inspector from Footings to Final. Record each Inspection Result, including Comments. Violations/Failed Inspections are Tracked to Resolution.

Process payments

Handling of all fees calculations and payment through TownSuite Finance

Subdivision Applications

Attachments

Fire inspections

Mapping/GIS

TownSuite® Mapping can interface with any municipal data, which stores AAN’s, PID’s or Civic address information. Mapping is also tightly connected to other TownSuite apps.

Comprehensive reporting

TownSuite has thousands of standard reports. All reports are criteria driven, meaning you can produce numerous variations of the same report depending on your needs.

Process management

All processes are fully customizeable and not restricted by the system. This helps ensure processes such as permits comply with existing zoning regulations.

Connected

Connect your information, your staff and your departments to information that is always up to date.

Priority support

Our technical support puts your first. Our team has a depth of municipal accounting experience that ensures you get the solutions you need.
