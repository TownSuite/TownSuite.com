TownSuite Edge 2020

We are excited to introduce our inaugural user conference, TownSuite Edge. This event will be held from May 20-21, 2020 in historic St. John's, NL.

Please check back regularly for additional information about this exciting event. All registered participants will also receive new information and updates via email.

Please note that all registrants will be required to have their own eServices account with TownSuite Municipal Software. To create this account please see your designated eBilling contact and ask them to create an account for you. Not sure how to do that? All the information you need to setup an eServices account is available right here. Questions? Comments? Feel free to contact us for more information.
